## Account Privacy Settings

### Make Your Account Private
**Navigation:** Profile → ☰ (Menu) → Settings → Privacy → Account Privacy  
**Steps:**
1. Tap your profile picture (bottom right)
2. Tap ☰ (three lines, top right)
3. Tap "Settings and privacy"
4. Tap "Account privacy"
5. Toggle "Private account" to ON (switch turns blue/green)

**What it does:**
- Only approved followers can see your posts, stories, and reels
- Your profile won't appear in Explore or hashtag searches to non-followers
- People must send follow requests that you can approve/deny
- Existing followers remain unless you remove them
- **Recommended:** Enable this for maximum privacy

**Platform Differences:**
- iOS: Toggle appears blue when enabled
- Android: Toggle appears green/blue when enabled
- Functionality is identical on both platforms

---

### Control Who Can Tag or Mention You
**Navigation:** Profile → ☰ → Settings → Privacy → Tags and mentions  
**Steps:**
1. Go to Settings (via profile menu ☰)
2. Tap "Privacy"
3. Tap "Tags and mentions"
4. Under "Allow tags from," tap and select:
   - **Everyone** (not recommended)
   - **People you follow** (moderate privacy)
   - **No one** (maximum privacy)
5. Under "Allow mentions from," tap and select:
   - **Everyone** (not recommended)
   - **People you follow** (moderate privacy)
   - **No one** (maximum privacy)
6. Toggle "Manually approve tags" to ON (recommended)

**What it does:**
- Controls who can tag you in their photos/videos/reels
- Controls who can @mention you in comments/captions/stories
- Manual approval lets you review tags before they appear on your profile
- Tagged content can expose your profile to unwanted audiences
- **Recommended:** Set both to "People you follow" + enable manual approval

---

### Hide Your Story from Specific People
**Navigation:** Profile → ☰ → Settings → Privacy → Story  
**Steps:**
1. Go to Settings → Privacy → Story
2. Tap "Hide story from"
3. Search for and select people you want to hide stories from
4. Tap "Done" (iOS) or checkmark (Android)

**Alternative quick method:**
1. Open your Story
2. Tap "👁 [number] viewers"
3. Next to any viewer, tap the three dots (⋯)
4. Tap "Hide story from [name]"

**What it does:**
- Selected people won't see ANY of your stories
- They won't be notified that they're hidden
- Useful for hiding from specific followers without unfollowing
- They can still see your regular posts (unless account is private)
- **Recommended:** Hide from acquaintances, coworkers, or family as needed

---

### Control Who Can Share Your Posts to Stories
**Navigation:** Profile → ☰ → Settings → Privacy → Story → Allow resharing  
**Steps:**
1. Settings → Privacy → Story
2. Scroll to "Allow resharing to Stories"
3. Toggle OFF (gray)

**What it does:**
- Prevents others from adding your feed posts to their stories
- Limits the spread of your content beyond your immediate followers
- Applies to all future posts
- **Recommended:** Turn OFF for maximum content control

## Activity & Visibility Controls

### Hide Your Activity Status
**Navigation:** Profile → ☰ → Settings → Privacy → Activity status  
**Steps:**
1. Settings → Privacy → Activity status
2. Toggle "Show activity status" to OFF (gray)

**What it does:**
- Hides the green "Active now" dot on your profile
- Hides "Active X minutes ago" in DMs
- Others won't see when you were last online
- **Trade-off:** You also won't see others' activity status
- **Recommended:** Turn OFF for privacy

---

### Control Comment Filtering
**Navigation:** Profile → ☰ → Settings → Privacy → Comments  
**Steps:**
1. Settings → Privacy → Comments
2. Enable "Hide offensive comments" (uses AI filtering)
3. Enable "Manual filter"
4. Tap "Manual filter" to add custom keywords
5. Type words/phrases to block, tap "+" after each
6. Scroll down to "Block comments from"
7. Select accounts whose comments you want to block

**What it does:**
- **Hide offensive comments:** Auto-filters using Instagram's AI
- **Manual filter:** Blocks specific words/phrases you define (e.g., spam keywords)
- **Block comments from:** Hides all comments from specific accounts
- Comments are hidden but not deleted
- **Recommended:** Enable all filtering options, add common spam phrases

### Control Who Can See Your Following/Followers Lists

## Story & Content Privacy

**Steps:**
1. Tap profile, then ☰ menu
2. Tap "Close Friends"
3. Search for and select people
4. Tap "Add" next to their names
5. Tap "Done" when finished

**How to share story with Close Friends only:**
1. Create a story as normal
2. Before sharing, tap "Close Friends" (green circle)
3. Story is visible only to this list

**What it does:**
- Creates a curated list of your closest contacts
- Share stories only with this group
- Marked with a green circle (Close Friends) vs. purple (public story)
- People aren't notified when added/removed
- **Recommended:** Use for personal content instead of hiding from many people

**What it does:**
- Removes the tag linking to your profile

- You won't get notifications about it
- Tag creator isn't notified
- **Recommended:** Regularly review and remove unwanted tags

## Messages & Interactions

### Filter Message Requests
**Navigation:** Profile → ☰ → Settings → Privacy → Messages  
**Steps:**
1. Settings → Privacy → Messages
2. Under "Message controls," toggle "Hide more message requests" to ON
3. Under "Others on Instagram," select:
   - **People you follow on Instagram** (recommended)
   - **Everyone on Instagram** (not recommended)
4. Under "Accounts you don't follow," configure:
   - **Don't receive requests** (maximum privacy)
   - **Receive requests from everyone** (not recommended)

**What it does:**
- **Hide more message requests:** Auto-filters low-quality/spam DMs
- **Others on Instagram:** Controls who can send you regular DMs
- **Accounts you don't follow:** Extra filtering for unknown senders
- Filtered messages go to "Message Requests" folder
- **Recommended:** Enable "Hide more" and restrict to "People you follow"

---

### Block an Account
**Navigation:** Profile page → ⋯ → Block  
**Steps:**
1. Go to the person's profile
2. Tap ⋯ (three dots, top right)
3. Tap "Block"
4. Choose:
   - **Block [username]** (complete block)
   - **Block [username] and new accounts they may create** (stronger)
5. Confirm

**What it does:**
- They can't see your profile, posts, or stories
- They can't message or tag you
- You won't see their content
- Existing messages remain but they can't send new ones
- They're removed from your followers
- **They won't receive a notification** but may notice
- **Recommended:** Use for harassment, spam, or unwanted contact

---

### Restrict an Account (Softer than Block)
**Navigation:** Profile page → ⋯ → Restrict  
**Steps:**
1. Go to the person's profile
2. Tap ⋯ (three dots)
3. Tap "Restrict"
4. Confirm

**Alternative method:**
1. Long-press their comment on your post
2. Tap "Restrict"

**What it does:**
- **Comments:** Their comments on your posts are only visible to them (you can approve to make public)
- **DMs:** Their messages go to requests, not your main inbox
- **Activity status:** They can't see when you're active
- **Read receipts:** They can't see if you've read their DMs
- **They won't know they're restricted** (appears normal to them)
- **Recommended:** Use for soft blocking without confrontation

---

### Mute Accounts (Without Unfollowing)
**Navigation:** Profile page → Following → Mute  
**Steps:**
1. Go to the person's profile
2. Tap "Following"
3. Tap "Mute"
4. Choose what to mute:
   - **Posts** (won't appear in your feed)
   - **Stories** (won't appear in story bar)
   - **Notes** (won't appear in DM thread)
5. Select duration: Until you unmute / For 24 hours / For 7 days

**What it does:**
- Hides their content without unfollowing
- They won't be notified
- Useful for taking a break from someone without social awkwardness
- **Recommended:** Use instead of unfollowing for acquaintances

## Accounts Center Privacy Settings

> **About Accounts Center:** Meta's centralized hub for managing privacy settings across Instagram, Facebook, Messenger, and other Meta platforms. Settings here often override individual app settings.

**How to Access:** Profile → ☰ (Menu) → Settings and privacy → Accounts Center

---

### Personal Details & Visibility (Accounts Center)
**Navigation:** Profile → ☰ → Settings → Accounts Center → Personal details  
**Steps:**
1. Tap profile → ☰ → Settings and privacy
2. Tap "Accounts Center"
3. Tap "Personal details"
4. Review and manage:
   - **Contact info:** Email, phone number (tap to edit or remove)
   - **Name:** Your display name across Meta accounts
   - **Birthday:** Date of birth (tap "Who can see your birthday" to control visibility)
   - **Gender:** How you identify (tap to make private)
5. For each item, tap "Who can see your [detail]" and select:
   - **Only you** (most private)
   - **Friends** (moderate)
   - **Public** (not recommended)

**What it does:**
- Controls visibility of personal information across all Meta accounts
- Email and phone can be used to find your account (hide if you want privacy)
- Birthday visibility affects who sees it on your profile
- **Recommended:** Set all to "Only you" for maximum privacy

---

### Sharing Across Profiles (Accounts Center)
**Navigation:** Profile → ☰ → Settings → Accounts Center → Sharing across profiles  
**Steps:**
1. Settings → Accounts Center → Sharing across profiles
2. Review and disable the following:

**Story Sharing:**
- Tap "Story sharing"
- Toggle OFF "Share to Facebook" (prevents automatic cross-posting)
- Toggle OFF "Share to Messenger" if available

**Reels Sharing:**
- Tap "Reels sharing"
- Toggle OFF "Recommend on Facebook" (keeps reels Instagram-only)

**Profile Info Sharing:**
- Tap "Profile sharing" or "Connected experiences"
- Toggle OFF all cross-platform connections

**Activity Sharing:**
- Look for "Share activity between accounts"
- Toggle OFF to prevent activity syncing

**What it does:**
- Prevents automatic cross-posting to Facebook
- Stops Meta from syncing your Instagram activity to Facebook
- Keeps your Instagram and Facebook identities separate
- Reduces data correlation across platforms
- **Recommended:** Disable all sharing across profiles

---

### Your Activity Off Meta Technologies
**Navigation:** Profile → ☰ → Settings → Accounts Center → Your information and permissions → Your activity off Meta technologies  
**Steps:**
1. Settings → Accounts Center
2. Tap "Your information and permissions"
3. Tap "Your activity off Meta technologies"
4. Review the list of apps and websites tracking you
5. Tap "Clear history" to disconnect past activity
6. Tap "Manage future activity"
7. Toggle OFF "Future off-Meta activity"
8. Select "Disconnect future activity" for Instagram (and Facebook if connected)

**What it does:**
- Shows apps/websites with Meta Pixel or SDK that track you
- Examples: shopping sites, news sites, apps with Facebook login
- Clearing history prevents Instagram from linking past browsing to your account
- Disabling future activity stops new tracking
- **Important:** This is one of the BIGGEST privacy settings
- **Recommended:** Clear history monthly and keep future activity OFF

**What's tracked without this:**
- Websites you visit with Meta Pixel
- Products you view/purchase on other sites
- Apps you use that integrate Facebook/Instagram SDKs
- Links you click in Instagram (tracked via in-app browser)

### Ad Preferences (Accounts Center)
**Navigation:** Profile → ☰ → Settings → Accounts Center → Ad preferences  
**Steps:**
1. Settings → Accounts Center → Ad preferences
2. Tap "Ad settings"
3. Review and disable:
   - **Data about your activity from partners:** Toggle OFF
   - **Categories used to reach you:** Tap, review lists uploaded by advertisers, hide unwanted
   - **Advertisers:** Tap, hide specific advertisers you don't want targeting you
   - **Ad topics:** Select topics to "See less" of
4. Tap "How ads work"
5. Review but note: can't fully disable ads, only limit personalization

**Additional settings:**
- **Audience-based advertising:** If available, toggle OFF
- **Ads based on partnerships:** Toggle OFF
- **Ads using your activity information:** Limit or disable if option available

**What it does:**
- Limits ad targeting based on data from third-party partners
- Prevents advertisers from using uploaded customer lists to find you
- Reduces personalized/targeted advertising
- Makes ads more generic (still shows ads, just less creepy)
- **Recommended:** Disable all partner data and audience-based advertising

### Account Status and Removal (Accounts Center)
**Navigation:** Profile → ☰ → Settings → Accounts Center → Personal details → Account ownership and control  
**Steps:**
1. Settings → Accounts Center → Personal details
2. Scroll down to "Account ownership and control"
3. Options available:

**Deactivation:**
- Tap "Deactivation or deletion"
- Select "Deactivate account"
- Choose Instagram
- Select reason
- Confirm (account is hidden but not deleted, can reactivate anytime)

**Deletion:**
- Tap "Deactivation or deletion"
- Select "Permanently delete account"
- Choose Instagram
- Enter password
- Confirm (account deleted after 30-day grace period)

**Download Before Deleting:**
- Always download your data first (see below)
- Deletion is permanent after grace period

**What it does:**
- **Deactivate:** Temporarily hides profile, posts, comments (reversible)
- **Delete:** Permanently removes account after 30 days (irreversible)
- **Recommended:** Deactivate first to test, delete only if sure

## Data & Personalization

### Download Your Data (Accounts Center)
**Navigation:** Profile → ☰ → Settings → Accounts Center → Your information and permissions → Download your information  
**Steps:**
1. Tap profile → ☰ → Settings
2. Tap "Accounts Center" at the bottom
3. Tap "Your information and permissions"
4. Tap "Download your information"
5. Select "Instagram" account
6. Tap "Request a download"
7. Choose date range (All time or specific range)
8. Choose format: HTML (readable) or JSON (raw data)
9. Tap "Submit request"
10. Wait for email notification (can take up to 48 hours)
11. Return to this menu and tap "Download" when ready

**What it does:**
- Downloads ALL your Instagram data:
  - Posts, stories, reels, IGTV videos
  - Comments, likes, saved posts
  - Messages (DMs)
  - Search history, account settings
  - Ads you've interacted with
  - Your activity off Instagram (if tracked)
- Helps you understand what Instagram has stored
- **Recommended:** Download annually to audit your data

## Security Settings

### Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)
**Navigation:** Profile → ☰ → Settings → Security → Two-factor authentication  
**Steps:**
1. Settings → Security → Two-factor authentication
2. Tap "Get started"
3. Choose security method:
   - **Authentication app** (most secure - Google Authenticator, Authy, etc.)
   - **Text message** (less secure, can be intercepted)
4. Follow setup instructions:
   - **For app:** Scan QR code or enter code manually
   - **For SMS:** Enter phone number, verify code
5. Save backup codes in a secure location (password manager or printed)
6. Tap "Done"

**What it does:**
- Requires a second verification code when logging in from new devices
- Prevents unauthorized access even if password is stolen
- **Authentication app is MUCH more secure** than SMS (SIM swapping risk)
- **Recommended:** Enable with authentication app (not SMS)

---

### Review Login Activity
**Navigation:** Profile → ☰ → Settings → Security → Login activity  
**Steps:**
1. Settings → Security → Login activity
2. Review all active sessions:
   - Device type (iPhone, Android, Web)
   - Location (city, country)
   - Last active time
3. Tap any suspicious login
4. Tap "This wasn't me" if unauthorized
5. Follow prompts to secure account

**What it does:**
- Shows all devices/locations where you're logged in
- Helps detect unauthorized access
- "This wasn't me" logs out the device and prompts password change
- **Recommended:** Review monthly and remove unknown sessions

---

### Change Password
**Navigation:** Profile → ☰ → Settings → Security → Password  
**Steps:**
1. Settings → Security → Password
2. Enter current password
3. Enter new strong password:
   - 12+ characters
   - Mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, symbols
   - Unique to Instagram (don't reuse)
4. Confirm new password
5. Tap "Save" (iOS) or checkmark (Android)

**What it does:**
- Updates account password
- Protects against credential stuffing attacks
- All other sessions remain logged in (unless you log them out)
- **Recommended:** Change every 3-6 months with unique password

---

### Set Up Login Requests (Extra Security Layer)
**Navigation:** Profile → ☰ → Settings → Security → Two-factor authentication → Login requests  
**Steps:**
1. After enabling 2FA, go to Security → Two-factor authentication
2. Scroll to "Login requests"
3. Toggle ON "Require login requests on unknown devices"

**What it does:**
- When logging in from new device, you must approve on a trusted device
- Adds extra layer beyond 2FA codes
- Prevents login even if someone has your password + 2FA code
- **Recommended:** Enable if available (may require authentication app 2FA)

## iOS-Specific Privacy Settings

### Disable Instagram's Access to iOS Features
**Navigation:** iPhone Settings (not Instagram app) → Instagram  
**Steps:**
1. Open iOS **Settings** app (not Instagram)
2. Scroll down to "Instagram"
3. Review and disable unnecessary permissions:
   - **Location:** Change to "Never" or "Ask Next Time"
   - **Contacts:** Disable if you don't want Instagram to access contacts
   - **Camera:** Required for taking photos in-app (usually keep enabled)
   - **Microphone:** Required for videos/voice messages (usually keep enabled)
   - **Photos:** Change to "Selected Photos" instead of "All Photos"
   - **Tracking:** Disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track"

**What it does:**
- **Location:** Prevents Instagram from tracking your location
- **Contacts:** Prevents "Find Friends" feature from uploading contacts
- **Photos:** Limits which photos Instagram can access
- **Tracking:** Prevents Instagram from tracking you across other apps/websites (iOS 14.5+)
- **Recommended:** Disable location, set photos to "Selected", deny tracking

---

### Use iOS App Privacy Report
**Navigation:** iPhone Settings → Privacy & Security → App Privacy Report  
**Steps:**
1. iOS Settings → Privacy & Security
2. Tap "App Privacy Report"
3. Toggle "Turn On App Privacy Report"
4. Wait 24-48 hours for data collection
5. Return and review:
   - **Data & Sensor Access:** How often Instagram uses camera, microphone, location
   - **App Network Activity:** Domains Instagram contacts (facebook.com, etc.)

**What it does:**
- Shows exactly what Instagram accesses on your device
- Reveals tracking domains and data sharing
- Helps you make informed privacy decisions
- **Recommended:** Enable to audit Instagram's behavior

### Disable WiFi Network Tracking (iOS)
**Navigation:** iPhone Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services → System Services  
**Steps:**
1. iOS Settings → Privacy & Security
2. Tap "Location Services"
3. Scroll to bottom, tap "System Services"
4. Disable:
   - **Wi-Fi Networking** (prevents location tracking via WiFi networks)
   - **Networking & Wireless** (limits network-based tracking)
5. For Instagram specifically, go back to Location Services
6. Find "Instagram"
7. Select "Never" or "Ask Next Time Or When I Share"
8. Toggle OFF "Precise Location"

**What it does:**
- Prevents iOS from using WiFi networks to determine your location
- Instagram can't access WiFi-based location data
- Disabling precise location gives approximate location only (city-level)

## Troubleshooting

**"I can't find a setting mentioned here"**
- Instagram frequently moves settings
- Use search bar in Settings (magnifying glass icon)
- Some settings may be region-restricted

**"Someone can still see my stories after I hid them"**
- They may have a secondary account not on your hide list
- Check "Hide story from" list to confirm they're added
- Consider making account fully private instead

**"I'm locked out after enabling 2FA"**
- Use backup codes saved during 2FA setup
- Request login code via SMS (if enabled)
- Contact Instagram support via "Need more help?" on login screen

**"Third-party app won't let me log in"**
- Instagram restricted API access for many apps
- Use Instagram's native features instead
- Remove app from authorized list if no longer working
